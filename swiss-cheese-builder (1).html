Understanding the Swiss Cheese Model

James Reason's Swiss Cheese Model shows how failures in complex systems like healthcare are rarely caused by a single error. Instead, multiple system weaknesses (holes) must align for an adverse event to occur.

🔴 Active Failures

Unsafe acts by people in direct contact with patients (slips, lapses, mistakes)

⚠️ Latent Conditions

Hidden system weaknesses that lie dormant (poor design, training gaps)

🛡️ Defenses

Barriers that prevent errors reaching patients (checks, protocols, alarms)

➡️ Trajectory

Path through aligned holes that allows hazard to cause harm
