üí° SBAR Best Practice Tips
  • Be specific: Use exact patient identifiers, room numbers, and measurements
  • Stay objective: Report facts and observations, not interpretations in S and B
  • Think clinically: Show your reasoning in Assessment section
  • Be clear about urgency: Make your timeframe expectations clear in Recommendation
  • Practice brevity: Concise communication reduces errors and improves response
