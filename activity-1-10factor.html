Welcome to Your Sustainability Assessment

This tool helps you evaluate your quality improvement project against the NHS Sustainability Model's 10 critical factors.

Rate each factor to identify strengths and areas needing attention before implementation.

Green: Ready
Amber: Developing
Red: At Risk

Time required: 7-10 minutes

Your assessment will be saved and you can download a PDF summary at the end.

Leadership Factors

0/3
Senior Leadership
Executive sponsors who actively champion your improvement, allocate resources, and remove barriers.
Clinical Leadership
Clinical champions who drive adoption, credibly advocate for change, and influence colleagues.
Fit with Organisational Strategy
Alignment with strategic priorities, board objectives, and organisational transformation agenda.

Process Factors

0/4
Infrastructure
Systems, technology, equipment, and physical environment support the improvement.
Staff Involvement
Frontline staff co-designed the improvement and feel ownership over its success.
Resources
Dedicated funding, protected time, and sufficient staffing to maintain the improvement.
Monitoring & Feedback
Regular data collection, performance dashboards, and feedback loops to track progress.

People Factors

0/3
Training & Skills
Staff have the knowledge, skills, and ongoing training to deliver the improvement effectively.
Behaviour Change
The improvement becomes embedded in routine practice and organisational culture.
Spread Beyond Site
Potential for replication in other departments, sites, or organisations.
