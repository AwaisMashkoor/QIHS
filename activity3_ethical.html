She becomes extremely distressed when isolated in her room and repeatedly attempts to leave, increasing her fall risk. Identify Ethical Principles at Stake

Rate the relevance of each principle to this scenario (1 = low, 5 = high):

Autonomy

Respecting Mrs. Thompson's wishes, preferences, and right to make decisions about her own care.

Beneficence

Acting in Mrs. Thompson's best interests, promoting her wellbeing and quality of life.

Non-maleficence

Avoiding harm to Mrs. Thompson (physical falls, psychological distress) and others (infection risk).

Justice

Fairness to all residents, equitable distribution of resources, and protecting vulnerable populations.
