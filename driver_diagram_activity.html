Activity 2: Interactive Driver Diagram Builder

Build Your Driver Diagram

Driver diagrams help you map out the factors that influence your improvement aim. You'll identify:

  • Aim: What you're trying to achieve
  • Primary Drivers: Major factors that directly influence your aim (add 3-4)
  • Secondary Drivers: Specific elements that support each primary driver (add 2-3 per primary)

Make it SMART: Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound
