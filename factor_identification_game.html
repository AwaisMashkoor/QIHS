📊 Factor Identification Activity

Drag and drop scenarios to the correct human factors category

HLTH09013 - Interactive Learning Exercise

📱 Mobile users: Tap and hold to drag items, or tap to select and then tap the destination zone

🎯 Instructions

Below you'll find real healthcare scenarios. Your task is to identify which type of human factor contributed most significantly to each situation. Drag each scenario to the correct category:

  • 🧠 Cognitive Factors: Mental processes like attention, memory, decision-making
  • 👥 Social Factors: Team dynamics, communication, hierarchy, culture
  • 🏢 Organizational Factors: Policies, resources, training, work environment

🎭 Healthcare Scenarios

1
Interrupted Focus: A nurse forgets to document medication administration after being interrupted by a phone call during the process.
2
Hierarchy Challenge: A junior doctor notices a consultant prescribing an unusually high dose but doesn't speak up due to fear of questioning authority.
3
System Design Flaw: Two different medications have nearly identical packaging and names, leading to frequent mix-ups despite staff being careful.
4
Decision Under Pressure: During a code blue, a physician makes a dosing error while trying to quickly calculate medication amounts under extreme time pressure.
5
Communication Breakdown: Important patient information is lost during shift handover because the outgoing nurse uses unclear terminology that the incoming nurse misunderstands.
6
Resource Shortage: A patient falls while trying to reach the bathroom because staffing shortages mean nurses cannot respond to call bells promptly.
🧠 Cognitive Factors
Mental processes: attention, memory, decision-making, cognitive biases
👥 Social Factors
Interpersonal dynamics: teamwork, communication, hierarchy, culture
🏢 Organizational Factors
