⚖️ Culture Shift: From Blame to Just

A medication error occurs. How does your organisation respond?

🚫 Blame Culture Response
✅ Just Culture Response

🚫 Blame Culture Path

  • Nurse suspended → Investigation → Disciplinary
  • Fear and defensiveness spread
  • Incidents hidden rather than reported
  • Errors repeat because system flaws remain unexamined

Outcome: A culture of fear and recurring harm.

✅ Just Culture Path

  • Team huddle after the incident
  • Focus on system analysis and contributing factors
  • Learning shared openly across the team
  • Preventative measures introduced to strengthen defenses

Outcome: A culture of openness, learning, and continuous improvement.
